Epics Project

Mental health illness pose a significant global public health threat, exacerbated by the lack of effective early identification and intervention measures. This project aims to address these challenges by focusing on emotion recognition in homeless children. Who are particularly vulnerable due to the loss of their parents. We develop a mobile application that screens mental health status by analyzing responses to screening questions through AI-driven emotion recognition. This approach make it more accessible and effective for caretakers in orphan homes. By providing clear guidance based on their results, we hope to ensure timely care for at-risk children, ultimately improving their mental health status..
